SUMMARY: WEATHER PLOTS
Latitude vs. Max Temperature

This project aimed to (1) use the OpenWeatherMap API to randomly select 500 or more cities across the world of varying distances from the equator and (2) create a series of scatterplots and regressions to examine the relationship between weather-related variables.


Matplotlib and Scipy were used to visualize and showcase the relationships. Here we analyze the relationships between latitude with temperature, and wind speed. In each analysis, we compare whether hemisphere is a potential effect modifer in these relationships.


The associations between the variables of interest were described in detail. For example, the association depicted here shows a curvilinear relationship between the city's maximum temperature and its latitude. However, this association does not take into account the hemisphere of the city. Overall, these data suggest that hemisphere matters.
